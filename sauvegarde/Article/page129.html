Your Kai skill enables you to identify these plants: they are Senara, exceedingly rare on Magnamund but seemingly abundant here in Vhozada. The unripened buds of this herb have great restorative qualities and when eaten will enable you to regain lost ENDURANCE points. Each Meal of Senara will restore 3 ENDURANCE points and there are enough buds in this field for over 100 Meals.If you wish to keep any of the Senara buds, remember to make the necessary adjustments to your Action Chart.
